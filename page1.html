Working in groups

Arguments against working in a small group

Disagreements may arise in the group which may result in the task taking longer or not being completed. Difficult to find a time to meet that suits everyone in the group. There are also people in the group who have jobs, which makes it even more difficult to meet. It may happen that one person does all the work, and that the rest do not contribute so much. it can create irritation that will affect the group members' willingness to cooperate.

Arguments in favor of working in a small group

Benefits with working in a small group is that you can learn from each other and learn how to work together. People will also have different opinions on the task, so the group can discuss each idea to find the advantages and disadvantages. That makes it easier to find the best ideas. Another benefit is that you can split the task and each member can work on their own part. In that way it's easier to solve it faster, and each part can be answered more thoroughly. Also, in a small group it can be easier to have effective communicsation with all of the group members.
